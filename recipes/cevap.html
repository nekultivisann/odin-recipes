Cevap

Description

Cevapi, or Cevapcici, are homemade sausages traditional of the Balkans area. This delicious street food made with mixed meats, is perfect for a party in your garden: tasty and easy to prepare thanks to simple ingredients

The Serbian Journalist and Novelist Branislav Nušić (1864-1938) affirmed that the Cevapi was an idea of a man called Zivko, owner of a family restaurant in Belgrade. In 1860, Zivko shaped the mixture of the Pljeskavica - the traditional Serbian hamburger - into a Kofta Keba

Ingredients

  • 1 part ground lamb
  • 1 part pork
  • 1 part beef
  • 1 part paprika
  • 1 part baking soda

How to make Cevapi

  1. Roll them into little sasauges
  2. Put some salt
  3. Cevapi can be baked on any surface, but barbecue is the best option
  4. Turn on, use a bit of oil, wait for tempreature to be high enough and put them on barbecue
  5. Cevapi will slowly get brown color, after 10 minutes or more, take them off
  6. Garnish with onion, bread and cream cheese
  7. Enjoy!!
